Levon

Gardening runs thick in my family, but my knowledge starts with how to water already established plants and ends with keeping an area weed free. Where I can't help on the phone, I'm able to make it so orders go out on-time and that the availability of our products is accurate. When I started working here 4 years ago I had no knowledge on starting seeds (thought you just threw them in the ground with water). Now I have a great respect for everything that goes into starting a seed and the people who do it. It brings me great joy knowing that each order I pack up and bring to the post office will bring happiness and enjoyment to peoples gardens. While my interests have shifted away from the gardens I grew up with to cars and video games, I do hope to one day be able to have a garden that I start from seed.
