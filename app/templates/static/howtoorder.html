How to Order

Online Orders (Secure Shopping Cart):

For your convenience, each section of our online catalog; Annuals, Perennials, Flowering Vines, Vegetables, and Herbs operates on our completely Secure Shopping Cart. To purchase an item, just click on the "ADD TO CART" button next to the item you wish to purchase. To view items in your cart or check out, click on the shopping cart icon in the upper right corner of every page. After completing the ordering process, you will receive a confirmation email which will include your order information and order number.

Fax Orders:

Payment:

We accept Visa, MasterCard and Discover for online orders with our Secure Shopping Cart. For mail orders, we also accept personal checks and money orders.

Shipping:

Standard shipping to the U.S. is $4.95.
Priority shipping to the U.S. is $9.95.

U.S. orders over $40.00 receive free shipping.
U.S. orders over $100.00 receive free Priority shipping.

International Shipping is $6.95.

All packages are shipped by the United States Postal Service.

Email Questions and Comments:

Email questions and comments are always welcome. We respond to most emails within 1 business day. Emails sent over the weekend are typically answered by the end of the next business day.
