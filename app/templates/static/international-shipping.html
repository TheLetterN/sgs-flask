International Shipping

We work hard to ensure all international seed orders are quickly processed and shipped. Our extensive experience shipping seed internationally helps shipments arrive without significant delays. Even with our experience and knowledge of shipping seed internationally, we recommend all of our international customers familiarize themselves with the current requirements of their country's ministry of agriculture.

Shipping Charges:

Standard international shipping is $6.95. Additional shipping charges may apply to international orders (excluding Canada) with a value greater than $200.00 USD. For orders shipping to Canada, additional shipping charges may apply to orders with a value greater than $400.00 USD. We will contact you before processing your order if any additional shipping charges apply.

Payment Options:

We accept Visa and Mastercard payments on our secure website. All charges are made in U.S. dollars. Some credit card companies require notification before international charges are made. We will contact you if we are unable to process your international payment.

Phytosanitary Certificates:

Most small, home-garden size seed orders shipped without a phytosanitary certificate arrive at their destination without an extra delay in customs. Please let us know if you require a phytosanitary certificate for your small, home-garden size seed order.

We require the purchase of a phytosanitary certificate for seed orders shipping internationally (excluding Canada) with a value greater than $500.00 USD. We do not require a phytosanitary certificate for orders shipping to Canada.

The cost for a phytosanitary certificate is $99.99 USD per shipment.
